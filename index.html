Easy

The goal of the game is to uncover all the cells that do not contain mines without being "blown up" by clicking on a cell with a mine underneath.

Left-click to reveal a cell.

Right-click to flag a cell.

Safe-Mode: Will mark a safe cell to click.

Hint-Mode: Left-click a cell to reveal all his neighbors for 1 sec.

Manually-Mode: Manually positioned mines and then play.

Undo: Go back one step in the game.
